Privacy Policy for Swappie

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Swappie ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application "Swappie" (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

When you register via Google or Apple Sign-In, we may collect:

  • Name and Email Address: Used for account management and communication.
  • Profile Picture: Displayed within your user profile.

B. User Content

We collect content you explicitly provide, including:

  • Recipes and Food Preferences: Recipes you create, import, or modify.
  • Dietary Restrictions: Information about allergies or diet goals (e.g., Keto, Vegan) to provide personalized recommendations.
  • Images: Photos of food you upload for AI analysis.

C. Google User Data (OAuth)

If you choose to connect your Google Account, we access the following based on your permissions:

  • Basic Profile (userinfo.profile, userinfo.email): To authenticate you and create your account.
  • Google Drive (drive.readonly): (Optional) Only requested if you choose to import recipes directly from your Google Drive documents. We do not read any files other than those you specifically select for import.

Limited Use Policy: Swappie's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered recipe swaps and nutritional analysis.
  • Manage your subscription and premium features.
  • Save your preferences and cookbooks across devices via our cloud database.
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI models (anonymized data only).

4. Third-Party Services

We may share data with the following trusted third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase: For secure user authentication and database hosting.
  • RevenueCat: For processing subscription payments. We do not store your credit card information.
  • Google Gemini AI: For processing recipe text and images to generate healthy alternatives.

5. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest within our database providers.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time within the App settings or by contacting us at mysimpleessential@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If you become aware of any data we have collected from children under 13, please contact us.
